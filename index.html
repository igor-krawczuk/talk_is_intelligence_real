Thesis Summary:  Intelligence is a bad concept and we should stop using it

Even if formulated bias-free it's

  1. ill posed
  2. coarse grained
  3. a-causal

On top of that it is

  1. inherently biased due to definition
  2. performative
  3. amenable for Bullshit,games & vacuous rhetoric

 

What we should do instead

  1. Spread knowledge (e.g. this talk :-)
  2. Focus on observable & controllable variables (i.e., remove inequalities)
  3. Let go of performative just-so stories
  4. Refuse god-of-the-gaps and vacuous counterfactual style reasoning
